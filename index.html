About font

HK Grotesk is a modern sans—serif font inspired by classic grotesques.
It's easy to read, it 's smooth and calm. It has balanced proportions with
a slight contrast in the thickness of the strokes. This gives the text a rhythm.
The lowercase letters are quite high relative to the uppercase ones.
The letters have soft rounded edges.
